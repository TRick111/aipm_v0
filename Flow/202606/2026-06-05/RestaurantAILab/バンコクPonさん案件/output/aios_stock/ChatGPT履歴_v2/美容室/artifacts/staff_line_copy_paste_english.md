# ✂️ **【LINEコピペ用｜Vietnam FOMO＋今月お客様が少ない理由まとめ（英語）】**

Here is a simple explanation about **why customer traffic is low this month** and **how FOMO can help us increase bookings**. Please read carefully.

---

**1. Why customer traffic is low this month (Vietnam-specific behavior)**  
This slow period is *normal* in Vietnam. There are strong seasonal and cultural reasons:

- **Pre-Tet Saving Period**  
  Many customers avoid coloring or big beauty spending in December–January because they save money for Tet.

- **Tet Beauty Rush**  
  Customers prefer to do their big beauty services in the **last 2–3 weeks before Tet**, not now.

- **Wedding Season**  
  Many weddings happen now, so customers avoid risky services like bleaching or major color changes.

- **Salary Cycle**  
  Vietnamese customers book more *after* payday (5th & 25th). Before these dates, traffic drops a lot.

- **Weather & Traffic Conditions**  
  Rainy days or heavy traffic weeks reduce walk-ins significantly.

👉 **This means the low traffic is NOT a salon problem. It is a normal seasonal pattern in Vietnam.**

---

**2. What is FOMO?**  
FOMO = **Fear of Missing Out**.  
Customers feel urgency when they think others are doing something they might miss.

Examples that trigger FOMO:  
- “Only 8 slots left this week”  
- “Tet bookings are almost full”  
- “Last year we had to reject 55 customers”  
- “This color is trending now on SNS”

FOMO messages make customers book *faster* and with *more confidence*.

---

**3. Why FOMO works especially well in Vietnam**  
- Vietnam has one of the **highest social media engagement rates** in Asia.  
- People follow what “others choose” (strong social proof culture).  
- Younger customers (20–35) react strongly to limited or exclusive offers.  
- “Limited slots” or “exclusive promotions” feel more valuable.

---

**4. Real success cases using FOMO**  
- **HCMC Nail Salon:** weekly remaining slots → customer count increased **2.3×**  
- **Singapore Color Salon:** limited 30 slots + daily updates → bookings **+1.7×**, revenue **+38%**  
- **Vietnam Aesthetic Clinic:** remaining doctor slots → revenue **+64%**

---

**5. What all successful salons did**  
- Show numbers (remaining slots / weekly bookings)  
- Add a clear reason for limits (Tet season / staff capacity / product supply)  
- Show social proof (trending colors, Before–After photos)  
- Use time pressure (“Valid until Feb 10”, “This week only”)
