“Like hair breathing—opening smoothly and closing softly.”

Like silk fluttering in the wind—light, smooth, and flexible.

### 🔹 Bleach

**Pattern ① (Basic)**  
👩‍🦰 Client: “I want to bleach my hair today.”  
✂️ Stylist: “Great! It’ll look beautiful. But bleach does put stress on the hair core.”  
👩‍🦰 Client: “So it will damage?”  
✂️ Stylist (calm, reassuring tone): “Yes, but with BG Treatment, we can protect it. It prevents breakage and split ends.”  
👩‍🦰 Client: “That sounds reassuring.”  
✂️ Stylist: “Exactly. Let’s bleach while caring for your hair so you can enjoy both brightness and longer-lasting results. Shall we add BG today?”  

---

**Pattern ② (Eggshell analogy)**  
👩‍🦰 Client: “Bleach damages hair, right?”  
✂️ Stylist (gentle tone, holding hands like an egg):  
“Yes, hair is like an egg—its protein (egg white) is protected by a shell. Bleach weakens the shell, but BG strengthens it.”  
👩‍🦰 Client: “So it protects?”  
✂️ Stylist (brighter tone): “Yes! It prevents breakage, so you can bleach safely. Shall we add BG today?”  

---

**Pattern ③ (House pillars analogy)**  
👩‍🦰 Client: “I want to bleach, but I’m worried about damage.”

👉 Gesture: clenched fist for strength

👩‍🦰 Client: “That makes sense.”  
✂️ Stylist (smiling tone): “Then let’s add BG so you can enjoy bleach safely.”
